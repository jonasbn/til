# Jira Rules of Engagement

This is not necessarily Jira specific, but most of my experience is with Jira due to long time exposure.

- No tickets are unsigned
- Tickets are assigned to the person who needs to take action, see also "Avoid depeche tickets", below
- Tickets are either open or closed

- Avoid depeche tickets, unless
  - You assign for assistance
  - You assign for review
  - You assign for clarification
  - You assign for test

- A ticket should be deployable
